# "Grow your money" slide deck

# Goal
I want to create a brief slide deck that's accessible to kids 9-12 that builds foundational understanding of how money can grow.

## Deck layout
1. Title Slide: "Grow Your Money!"
2. Grab their attention. "Wait, money can grow?!!?!", with mindblown gif.
3. What are stocks?
4. How stocks gain value
    - Examples of Netflix and Nvidia, including graphs of market cap and share price over time. Include specific values for price in say 2010, vs price today
5. Not all companies grow in value, and it's hard to know which will
    - don't put all your eggs in one basket. You could get super rich, but more than likely you'll lose money.
    - Balancing risk. 
    - Brief intro and plot of S&P500 growth over time.
6. Some fun / catchy wrap up slide with a good meme or gif

## Style choices
- Write this in first person, as if from my voice.
- Make this an HTML slide deck
- If necessary, add a simple submodule here to query share prices over time and visualize.
- Aim to make this engaging for the 9-12 age group.
